Method and Scope

Deep Mirror Protocol is based on narrative pattern analysis and cross-case synthesis.

The work draws from:

  • First-person accounts
  • Reported relational and institutional ruptures
  • Observable behavioral sequences
  • Structural similarities across otherwise unrelated cases

The protocol does not diagnose individuals, assign pathology, or propose treatment. It focuses on systems, not symptoms.

Interpretations are limited to what can be reasonably inferred from behavior, language, and sequence, rather than speculation about internal states.

The aim is clarity, not correction.
